Student Care
Our after-school student care incorporates the Project Approach to facilitate child-led exploration to help your child learn to learn, and take responsibility of their own learning journey.
Music
We use Teacher Ping's Baby Music programme to introduce children between 18 months to 4 years old to the wonderful world of music and body movement. Older children can look forward to violin and piano lessons in our music studios.
Dance
From classical ballet to hip-hop street dance, our experienced teachers help children build confidence through physical fitness and elegant body movements.
